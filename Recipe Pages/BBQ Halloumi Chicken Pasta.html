BBQ Halloumi Chicken Pasta


Serves 2 - Prep 10mins - Cook 30mins

  • 200g chicken breast 
  • BBQ seasoning 
  • Smoked Paprika  
  • 120g dry rigatoni 
  • Small red onion - diced 
  • Tsp garlic puree 
  • Tsp Oregano 
  • 60g halloumi  
  • 60g bbq sauce
  • 100ml chicken stock
  • 60g philadelphia
  • 50ml pasta water
  • Parsley to garnish

    • Download Ingredients List

Method

  1. Season the chicken with a tsp bbq seasoning, tsp of smoked paprika, salt and pepper. Rub in throughly on both sides of the breasts before baking in the oven for 20 minutes on 180 degrees.

  2. Pop the pasta on to boil in salted water.

  3. In a seperate pan, fry off the red onion in a tsp garlic puree. Whilst this is softening, dice the halloumi and start to pan fry.

  4. Add a tsp bbq seasoning, tsp of smoked paprika and the oregano to the pan with the onion in. Leave for a minute before adding the bbq sauce, chicken stock and cream cheese. Stir well add the pasta water and leave to simmer for 10 minutes on low-medium heat.

  5. Slice the chicken once cooked. Remove 2 tbsp of the bbq sauce, combine the rest of the sauce with the pasta.

  6. Plate up the pasta, top with the chicken, diced parsley and finish with the halloumi.
