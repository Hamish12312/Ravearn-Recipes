Raclette - Pudding


Serves 4 - Prep 10mins - Cook 30mins

  • 2-3 Bars of Chocolate (Dark, Milk or White)
  • Mini Doughnuts
  • Strawberrys
  • Raspberrys
  • Grapes
  • Banana

    • Download Ingredients List

Method

  1. Set up table with Raclette on a heatproof surface in the middle of the table. Clean if it was used for Dinner

  2. Break up chocolates into cubes and put in bowls around the raclette

  3. Put fruit and doughnuts into bowls, cut up where necerssary

  4. Heat up raclette and get started!
