Raclette


Serves 10 - Prep 20mins - Cook 1hour 30mins

  • 225g Plain Flour
  • 350g Caster Sugar
  • 85g Cocoa Powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp Bicarbonate of Soda
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 125ml Vegitable Oil
  • 250ml Milk
  • 250ml Boiling Water

    • Download Ingredients List

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180C and prepare 2 8 inch tins.

  2. Place all ingredients except boiling water into a bowl.

  3. Beat together until smooth.

  4. Add the boiling water a little at a time.

  5. Beat well, this will be very liquid.

  6. Pour into tins and bake for 25-35mins, 20mins for cupcakes

  7. Once baked, leave to cool and then fill with your filling of choice (Nutella or Chocolate Fudge Icing are good choices)
